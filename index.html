About Me

Hello! This is my personal website. I am a graduate of the Summer 2020 cohort of theClubhou.se code boot camp program, where I learned web development basics like working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL. After completing that program, I felt compelled to gain a deeper understanding of programming and software development and began attending Augusta Technical College, where I earned an A.A.S. in Computer programming and certifications in PHP and Cross-Platform Mobile development.


I also have experience in eLearning Development, creating learning content intended for synchronous and asynchronous delivery. I have created curriculum for organizations such as The Clubhou.se Code Boot Camp, The National Disaster Life Support Foundation, Evans High School Cross-Country Sports Organization, and more.
